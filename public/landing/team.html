EHR(Electronic health record)

Health Records, Anytime, Anywhere

At HealthiFam, we believe that managing your family’s health should be seamless and hassle-free. That’s why we offer our members access to Electronic Health Records (EHR)—a secure, efficient, and comprehensive way to keep track of your family's health information.

What is EHR?

Electronic Health Record (EHR) is a digital version of a patient’s paper chart. It includes the medical history of your family members, such as diagnoses, medications, treatment plans, immunization dates, allergies, radiology images, and laboratory test results. With HealthiFam's EHR system, all this crucial information is stored in one secure location, making it easily accessible whenever you need it.
